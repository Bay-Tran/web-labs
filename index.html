Web Labs - Interactive simulations for active learning on the web

  • web-lab-3 - Feedback control of temperature during reaction in a continuous stirred tank reactor (CSTR)
  • web-lab-6 - Heat exchanger
  • web-lab-7 - Packed bed, plug flow reactor
  • web-lab-8 - Packed bed reactor with feed-product heat exchanger - combines units in labs 6 & 7
